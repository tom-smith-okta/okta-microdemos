This demo shows an example self-registration flow and MFA enrollment with Okta, using the Okta widget.
What to do
  • Click on "login / register" to show the widget
  • At the bottom of the widget, you will see "Don't have an account? Sign up"
  • Click on "Sign up" to launch the self-registration form
  • Enter an email address with "@mfa.com" to trigger the MFA enrollment flow.
After registering and enrolling in MFA, you will have an Okta session.
For demo purposes, this user will be prompted for MFA every time they sign in, but this policy can of course be adjusted.
